# Big-Black-Gym-Log
A high-fidelity, gamified stat tracker built to integrate seamlessly with Torn's native UI.

Big Black Gym Log — Quick Start Guide

Getting Started: When you launch the script, you'll be guided through onboarding which explains the API key requirements and how your data is handled (stored securely in your browser). Before committing real data, test drive it in demo mode to explore all the features if you’d like.

History & Data: The script reaches back through your gym logs to find the last full day of training logs and starts tracking from there. While it can only access up to 100 gym entries from Torn’s API, this method  gives you the most comprehensive historical window possible.

Weekly Goals: Training is measured in Energy (E). Green days require 1,000E (2 xanax + all natural energy in a 24 hr period) and earn green jewels. Gold days require 1,500E (3 xanax + natural energy + daily refill) and earn gold bars. Green days are worth 1/5 of your weekly bar, gold days ~1/3. Hit 5 green days to fill the bar and earn 1 new sticker, or get 4 gold days (or 3 gold + 3 green) to fill it fully gold and earn 2 new stickers.

The Pan System: As the week progresses, your jewels and bars fill up in pans for each day. At week's end, the pans flip over, a random sticker from your pool of unlocked stickers is placed on the back, and each pan is spray-painted green or gold to permanently display your achievements.

Viewing Your Log: Use panel mode (footer bar) or full page mode (sidebar/top nav on mobile, attached to the Gym tab). Click day cells for daily stats, weekly bars for week summaries, or the crown button for all-time stats. The toolbar lets you switch between ledger view (table), graph view (visual stat trends), achievements (in development), and stickerbook (your sticker collection).
