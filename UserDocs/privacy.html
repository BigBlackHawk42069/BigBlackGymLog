What API Key is Needed?

Since the Torn API lacks a direct endpoint for training energy (E), this script requires an API key with log and battlestats access to read your training records. This is the only way to accurately calculate your energy spent. Without this level of access, the script’s core mechanics would not be possible.

API Call Frequency & Limits

This script makes 1 call when you click Train, and a 3-call reconciliation in three situations: when you leave the gym after a session, when you tap the manual Refresh button, and automatically in the background — no sooner than 2 hours after the last sync of any kind.

An optional Backfill feature is also available to import your full historical training data. Tapping the Backfill button opens a dedicated screen that fully explains how that process works and requires your explicit agreement before any scan begins.

How Your Data Is Handled

When accessing logs, this script only retrieves the specific training data for each stat — no other logs are read. All data retrieved from your API key is processed locally within your browser. This script does not transmit, store, or share your data externally in any way. I, as the developer, do not have access to your logs, API data, or any information generated by your use of this script.

Transparency & Verification

For full transparency, you can verify how your data is handled by reviewing the script's source code. Specifically, you can search for the section titled "THE CHECK-IN COUNTER," which clearly shows how and where data is processed. This allows you to independently confirm that all data remains on your device.
